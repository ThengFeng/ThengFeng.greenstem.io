Historical Milestones

1990

Journay Start

In 1990, Greenstem Business Software SDN.BHD was founded with a vision to To be number 1 software provider for automotive industry. Our journey began with a small team of dedicated individuals who shared a passion for IT.

1991

Dos-Based System

In the coming year, we are poised for success as we embark on the journey to develop a cutting-edge accounting system based on the foundation of our decades of experience with DOS technology. Our dedicated team is committed to creating an innovative solution that will redefine the way businesses manage their finances.

2000

Windows-Based System

In the year 2000, we achieved a significant milestone by successfully upgrading our system to a Windows-based platform. This transition marked a pivotal moment in our journey, allowing us to harness the full potential of modern technology and provide our clients with a more user-friendly, versatile, and efficient accounting solution. With this strategic move, we continued our commitment to innovation and ensured that our clients would benefit from the latest advancements in the field.

2020

Web-Base System

In 2023, we embarked on a transformative journey by transitioning to a web-based platform. This strategic shift allowed us to adapt to the evolving technological landscape, ensuring that our clients could access our accounting system from anywhere with an internet connection.
